Design Exercise
Short exercise designing an app used to manage a chain of physical therapy clinics.
User story: As a Physical Therapy Administrator, I need an easy way to track patient performance so that I can provide effective treatments, know what programs are in demand, and how my therapists are performing. This app assists in figuring out where to invest money, create additional programs, and essentially assess the health of the business.
Requirements:
  • 2 screens: Must show a mobile (iPhone) and desktop (1280x) view
  • Must show Market Share, # of therapists, visualizations for Gender breakdown, Age Breakdown, and Insurance Breakdown
